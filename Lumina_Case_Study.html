The Brief

A brand with great products
and a leaking checkout

Lumina Skincare came with a clear problem: a 74% cart abandonment rate and zero recovery infrastructure in place. Every customer who made it to checkout and didn't buy was gone forever — no follow-up, no second chance, no revenue recovered.

The Problem
74%

Cart abandonment with no recovery system

No follow-up emails. No welcome sequence. No second chance to convert warm, interested buyers. Revenue walking out the door, daily.

The Opportunity

Two precision-built sequences to plug the leak

A 5-part cart abandonment recovery flow and a 7-part welcome series — both engineered to turn hesitation into conviction, and browsers into buyers.

The Strategy

Three pillars.
Every email. No exceptions.

Rather than treating each email as an isolated send, every message in this sequence was architected around the same three conversion levers — layered differently depending on where the customer is in the journey.

01
Urgency

Real scarcity, expiring offers, and psychological triggers that move people from "I'll think about it" to "I need this now." No fake countdown timers — only honest, meaningful urgency that protects long-term brand trust.

02
Social Proof

Real customer voices, demographic-specific testimonials, and credibility signals that answer the one question every hesitant buyer is silently asking: "Does this actually work for someone like me?"

03
Brand Story

A founder-led narrative that builds emotional connection before asking for a sale. Customers don't buy products — they buy into people and missions they believe in. Every email earns trust before it asks for a conversion.
